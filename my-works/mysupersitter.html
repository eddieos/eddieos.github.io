The Problem

Connecting Baby Sitters with Parents

Seymour, a passionate entrepreneur, who aims to address the challenge of connecting babysitters and parents for seamless babysitting experiences. This is particularly relevant during the coronavirus pandemic, where there are concerns about verifying the vaccination status of both parties to avoid any conflicts.

What I did

User Flow, Wireframes and a High Fidelity Mock Up

In my role, I developed a style guide for our mobile app in order to keep consistency throughout the product. This began with understanding product requirements, user needs, and product goals, followed by creating a user flow. I then constructed wireframes for layout and functionality. The process continued with the creation of high-fidelity mock-ups, incorporating all final product elements. I consistently participated in ideation and brainstorming, striving to maintain relevance and elevate user experience in the evolving mobile app landscape.

Typography

The basic idea was to find a balance between the thin, wispy sans-serif used to indicate a ‘futuristic‘ tone, and a bold, masculine font synonymous with ‘construction‘. We came up with something in the middle, leaning towards lighter-weighted fonts, but still with a hint of that blocky ‘construction’ vibe. We use Chaney for general display and when we want to drive attention to the content, and the technical and geometric Sora font for the body copy and paste overall hierachy.
